Account Deletion Guide

Step-by-Step Instructions for Permanently Deleting Your Account (with Visual Aids)

⚠️ Important Warning: This action is irreversible and will remove all your data, including profile information, activity, and any associated content. Consider backing up anything important first.
1

Open the Profile Page

Launch the app and navigate to your profile. You'll see the header with the current time (e.g., "9:50 Good Evening") and profile details. Your profile photo (a circular avatar with an edit pencil icon) and name ("Dip user") should be displayed at the top.

2

Scroll to the Delete Option

Scroll down the profile page to view the available options. You'll see two main buttons: Log out (with a left-arrow icon and subtitle "Want to leave?") – This is for temporary sign-out; skip this. Delete Account (with a red-tinted user icon with an X and subtitle "Permanently remove your account and data") – Tap this red-highlighted option to proceed.

3

Confirm the Deletion in the Warning Dialog

Tapping "Delete Account" will open a pop-up modal (overlay) with a warning triangle icon (⚠️). The dialog title reads "**WARNING** Delete Account". It explains: "This action is irreversible. All your data will be permanently deleted. Are you sure?" At the bottom, you'll see two buttons: Cancel (gray button on the left) – Tap this if you change your mind. Delete Permanently (red button on the right) – Tap this to confirm.

4

Complete the Process

After tapping "Delete Permanently," the account will be deleted immediately. You may see a confirmation message or be redirected to the login screen. Once deleted, you won't be able to recover your data or log back in with this account. If you need to use the app again, you'll have to create a new account.
